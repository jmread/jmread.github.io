Bio

I am a professor in the DaSciM team of the Data Analytics and Machine Learning pole of the Computer Science Laboratory (LIX) at École Polytechnique, Institut Polytechnique de Paris. I work in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science and Mining.

  • 2019 — present. Professor (HDR 2017) at LIX, École Polytechnique, France.

  • 2016 — 2019. Assistant Professor at LIX, Ecole Polytechnique, France.

  • 2016 — 2016. Post-doc in INFRES, Télécom ParisTech, France.

  • 2013 — 2016. Post-doc at Aalto University, Finland.

  • 2010 — 2013. Post-doc (and in 2013, lecturer) in the Dept. TSC at the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, Spain.

  • 2010. PhD from the University of Waikato, New Zealand.

  • 2006. Bachelor of Computing and Mathematical Sciences (BCMS Hons.) from the University of Waikato, New Zealand.
