Bio

I am a professor in the DaSciM team in LIX at École Polytechnique, working in areas of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science and Mining.

  • Sep 2019 — Present Professor (Sep 2016 — Aug 2019 as Assistant Professor, HDR 2017) at LIX, École Polytechnique, France.

  • Feb 2016 — Aug 2016. Post-doc researcher at INFRES, Télécom ParisTech, France.

  • Dec 2013 — Jan 2016. Post-doc researcher at Aalto University, Finland.

  • Oct 2010 — Aug 2013. Post-doc (and Sep 2013 — Nov 2013 as lecturer) in the Dept. TSC at the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, Spain.

  • 2010. PhD (in the Machine Learning Group) from the University of Waikato, New Zealand.

  • 2006. Bachelor of Computing and Mathematical Sciences (BCMS Hons.) from the University of Waikato, New Zealand.
