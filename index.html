Bio

I am Professor in the DaSciM team in LIX at École Polytechnique. My research interests are in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science and Mining.

  • September 2016 — August 2019 Assistant professor and September 2019 — Present Professor in the DaSciM group at École Polytechnique in France.

  • February 2016 — August 2016. Post-doc researcher at Télécom ParisTech in France.

  • December 2013 — January 2016. Post-doc researcher affiliated with Aalto University and HIIT in Finland.

  • October 2010 — November 2013. Post-doc (and then visiting lecturer) in the Dept. of Signal Theory and Communications at the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid in Spain.

  • September 2010. Completed PhD in the Machine Learning Group at the University of Waikato in New Zealand.

  • 2006. Obtained Bachelor of Computing and Mathematical Sciences, (BCMS Hons.) at The University of Waikato, New Zealand.
