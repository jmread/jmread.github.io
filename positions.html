Undergraduate Projects (PRL) and Internships

Unfortunately, in general I do not take on undergraduate internship/research projects, with the exception of final-year projects, e.g., bachelor thesis (possibly leading on from a PRL) or 3A internship.

Masters Internships

If you are a Masters student, or if you have completed your Masters, looking for an internship, please let me know if you could be interested in some topics that I have been involved in. Especially, if you intend might wish to continue on to a PhD after the intership. You might find some here on the team’s website: ORAILIX.com
